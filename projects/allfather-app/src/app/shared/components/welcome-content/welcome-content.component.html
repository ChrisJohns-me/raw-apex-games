Welcome to

Allfather is an advanced overlay and app for Apex Legends.
  • Tracking: Saves your in-game stats, compiles, and presents them in meaningful ways, helping you to understand your playstyle better and improve strategies.
  • In-Game HUD: Monitors the killfeed, your inventory, your damage output, and your legend data to provide you with insightful and helpful features.

Getting Started

  1. Apex Legends (for PC) must be installed, either by Steam or Origin.
  2. Overwolf must be installed and running.
  3. Always open Allfather before opening Apex Legends.
    Your stats will only be tracked while Allfather is running.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is using Allfather cheating? Will I get banned?

A major concern of any player who uses a third-party app is whether or not it's allowed. The last thing a player wants is to use something that can get them banned by Respawn, Twitch, or other streaming services. Allfather for Apex Legends is 100% safe to use.

Here's why:
Any in-game application centers around the players' experience, and the goal is to improve a player's in-game experience by giving the player some type of advantage. Otherwise, why would the player use the app in the first place? The tricky thing is to find the thin line between having an advantage and cheating. With the possibilities to track almost any in-game event, we've only selected to track those events that are connected to YOUR current position, weapons, inventory, legends, and killfeed (which is visible in-game and to all players via the killfeed).
So how does Allfather benefit a player if the app only uses events that are already available to every player?

Allfather takes all of the visible in-game killfeed events and your in-game data, and provides you with insightful information based on that data.
Is that cheating? If so, I suppose a human with a better memory than another human is also cheating.

Our app has nothing that shows any type of hidden or specific information about the current match. Nor does it give an unfair advantage that could be considered cheating. Allfather is safe to use while streaming, playing pubs, and ranked games.
During development, we based our app on the Overwolf platform, which provides useful events and has years of development experience for popular competitive games. Many of the most famous game development studios like Valve, Blizzard, Riot, etc., allow the use of 3rd party apps.
The Overwolf platform has very strict and concise guidelines and always avoids any app that may even hint at cheating.
You can learn more about this here: https://medium.com/overwolf/how-overwolf-keeps-users-safe-from-bans-10f6a4657869

What game modes are supported?

Allfather currently only supports Battle Royale game modes.
This is due to Overwolf's lack of support for other game modes. We are continually working to add support when possible.
